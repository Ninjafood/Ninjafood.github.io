Questions, bug reports, or feature requests? Email synacksite@gmail.com and include your device model and iOS version. I aim to reply within a few days.

Frequently asked

  • Where do saved textures go? The Photos app, in an album called “Texture Ripper”.
  • How do I remove an edge point? Press & hold it, or double-tap it.
  • My texture looks skewed. Zoom in (pinch) and place the four corners precisely on the surface's corners — the loupe helps.
  • Does it work offline? Yes — everything except URL import.
