Texture Ripper — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Texture Ripper is designed so that your data stays yours. The short version: the app collects nothing.

Data collection

Texture Ripper does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Your photos

Photos you import are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere. Textures you save are written directly to your own photo library. Photo library and camera access are used only when you invoke them, solely for importing and saving images.

Network use

The app works offline. The only network activity occurs when you explicitly use “Import from URL”, which downloads the image you request directly to your device, and, rarely, to fetch an image-format decoder for unusual file types. No information about you is sent in either case.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and, consistent with the above, collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
